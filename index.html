What Is This

Modlab is a Kamloops based non-profit that is working to start a local makerspace.

Our goal is to establish and operate a communal shop where creative people can come together to share their enthusiasm and knowledge. The group is actively searching out new members and funding to help launch the space that will become our community workshop. Check our blog for progress updates or join the discussion and see if you can play a role in shaping your local makerspace!

Discussion

Please feel free to join us at community.modlab.ca and ask questions or voice your opinions. Community discussion is always encouraged and we're keen to hear what you think.

Funding

As Modlab is a non profit, all our funding is derived from the community. Memberships, donation, and sponsorships are what will give us the ability to run the makerspace. Please email us, or comment in the discussion group, if you have any funding leads for us.
